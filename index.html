about us

ByBikeX – become part of our common digital ecosystem, we encourage you, for a certain distance we plant trees, you ride - the planet becomes cleaner.

Let's make history together and green the planet!

Every year the number of emissions from cars and factories increases in the world, which entails irreparable harm to our planet, at such a pace we will only remember how beautiful our planet was.

According to Bloomberg analytical data, 92% of the world's population has ridden a bicycle at least once in their life - this allows us to make a statement about the availability and ease of use of this means of transportation.

We all use public transport, getting to work every day, or to visit friends, on average, the population of Europe spends 77 euros per month on public transport, which translates into 920 euros per year, and if we offer you to change to a bike and spend this one-time amount? What if we pay you for it?

ByBikeX – We plant a tree if you ride 100 kilometers, do you accept our challenge?

Our idea is to solve 3 problems - excess weight, saving money, protecting the environment.

We have created a unique project in synergy with blockchain technologies.

You woke up in the morning and are going to get to work, you can choose a car that requires constant maintenance and refueling, or a bus, which will cost almost 100 euros per month, but what about riding a bicycle - and we will pay you for it!

We have created a unique offer that combines cycling enthusiasts, professionals and everyone who wants to help the planet.

All you need is a smartphone, a bike and go!

We offer you the opportunity to create, buy a bike, rent or create your own bike, whatever you choose, we guarantee that you will get more. Our project has created the possibility of using it without investment, you can rent someone else's bike in the application and earn for yourself and the owner of BBX Coin.

ByBikeX aims to push millions of people to "green" (Spanish) the planet, we build the ideal world, green and clean.

After driving 100 kilometers, the user can plant a tree for free, but it is also possible to buy with their coins in order to plant a certain tree, for example, the user can pay 100 coins and an oak tree will be planted, for which the user will be rewarded with 5 coins per month, which he receives passively for the planted tree.
