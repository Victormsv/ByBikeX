Game process

ByBikeX is a smartphone app that allows you to record your bike rides with game elements, you can use the app for free to record your sports activities.

he ByBikeX app is a Play to earn game, with the possibility of earning money and improving the environment. The gameplay is based on owning a bike of one of the classes, which has distinctive characteristics and features, there are 4 classes in total:
- road bikes;
- Mountain bikes;
- city bikes;
- Stunt bikes. By choosing one of the available bike classes, you can start helping the environment and earn money by playing the ByBikeX game.

The application has 4 main tabs:
- Events - here you can find current tasks, and also get acquainted with the latest changes, or upcoming events in the ByBikeX universe;
- Garage - here you can find the bikes that you own or rent, see their modernization, check the wear and tear of the bikes and the energy available to you;
- Shop - this tab will allow you to purchase a new bike, upgrade or amplifier, as well as participate in the auction;
- Training - will allow you to record your sports activity, as well as convert it into earning coins and completing selected tasks;
- Community - You can find like-minded people, join communities or just share your results.

Starting a ride on a bike, the program calculates the possible number of coins for you after driving a certain distance for the energy available to you. For example, you have 10 energies, which takes 1 energy in 4 minutes, after driving 40 minutes on a bike, the energy will run out and the application will not count more coins for you, but will continue to record the indicators of your trip that you can see after it is completed. During the trip, you can pause 2 times, the duration of which in total does not exceed 1 hour. If you have traveled a distance and you have 1 energy left and you have completed the trip, the energy does not burn out and begins to be calculated for you, taking into account 1 remaining energy.

Only city-class bicycles can complete the trip with a mark of arrival at the destination, and continue their trip again during the day, during the stop of the trip, such bikes do not burn out energy and do not accrue new energy, it remains in the amount that was at the time of arrival user to the destination. This function is available for 24 hours, after which the amount of energy will be frozen at the last mark and will be restored to full level for the next use.

Users can have up to 10 bicycles in the garage, but no more than 2 of the same class. If the user has several bikes, you can ride only one of the bikes available to you per day, the number of coins that the user can earn and the amount of energy that is available to the user will be changed relative to the bike you choose.

Bicycle classes and their distinguishing features:
1. Road bike - suitable for active cycling, has more energy than other bikes, suitable for overcoming long distances in one workout;
2. Mountain bike - suitable for users with average cycling activity, those who like to ride 2-5 times in the mountains or the forest on their bike, has increased wear;
3. City bike - suitable for users who have boldly decided to get to work or anywhere on their bike, this class has a feature. If you chose this bike in your garage in the morning to get to work, and rode a distance of 5 km. after using up half of the energy available to you, you can stop your ride and continue when you get home on it (this feature is only available on this type of bike);
4. Stunt bikes - suitable for users who like to perform daring stunts and show exciting moves, this bike has increased durability.

With in certain classes of bikes there is a monthly competition:
1. Road bike - a user who has ridden more kilometers in total in a month receives a random diamond or gold quality upgrade for his bike.
2. Stunt bikes - the user uploads their best bike trick in the app, and whose video gets the most views gets a random diamond or gold quality upgrade for their bike.
3. Mountain bikes - a user who has climbed to a great height in a total of a month receives a random upgrade for his bike from the quality of diamond or gold.

Аренда

Having bought a bike, the user can ride it himself and earn money, and can also rent out his bike by concluding a smart contract within the application itself.

User 1 (abbr. P1) bought a bike that generates 50 coins per day, but P1 cannot ride a bike that day and does not want to lose coins, he places an offer to rent a bike, P2 can rent it for this P1 will earn 30% of potential earnings, and P2 earns 65%, the remaining 5% goes to the site as a commission.

When renting a bike, it has the same wear and tear, the owner of the bike can rent it out once, as well as for a long time. When renting a bike for a long time, the user who rented it must independently maintain the bike.

You can rent out all the bikes he has in his garage. A user who rents from 2 bikes has the ability to change the percentage of earnings, for example, P1 can put 10 bikes and take 50% of the earnings. If the percentage of earnings changes upwards, the user who rents it will pay a commission to the site and pay for the wear of the bike by 50% with the user who rents the bike.

When entering into a smart contract for a specific period, users must adhere to the contract during the specified period of validity. If one of the parties wants to break it, the party that initiated the termination of the contract is charged with a fine in% of the number of coins earned by the user who rented it, and a fine in a fixed amount of coins for the user who leased the bike.

Events

Monthly marathons will be held in the application, for participation you only need a bike, it can be yours personally or rented by you, for participation you select one bike in advance without the possibility of further changing it.

Marathons will have a different character, like riding the longest distance on a bike in the allotted time, reaching the highest speed or gaining the highest height.

Various rewards will be drawn, coins, amplifiers, upgrades and bicycles.

Quests

The user can take every day, week, month for himself tasks such as "drive 1000 km in a month." for which the user will receive certain rewards. Tasks will be fixed for a week and a month, limited per day.

Passing quests, the user will earn coins, as well as open upgrades for his bike.
